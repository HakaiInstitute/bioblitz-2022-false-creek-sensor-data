# bioblitz-2022-false-creek-sensor-data
Temperature logger data from the 2022 False Creek Bioblitz

## Summary
Temperature data collected from Tidibt MX2300 loggers associated with settlement plates deployed for the 2022 False Creek Bioblitz run by the Hakai Institute. 

## Methods
Loggers were suspended by ropes 0.5 metres below the surface from floating docks in proximity to settlement plates which were located at floating docks across False Creek. The loggers were programmed to start logging sea surface temperature every 10 minutes on July 12th 2022 at 12 pm until the memeory space is used up. Loggers were removed from the water in early September 2022 when the settlement plates were retrieved. They are deployed at 0.5 metres below the water surface. 

Once retrieved from the water: 
- Data were downloaded from sensors via bluetooth
- Data were exported using hoboconnect in .hobo format and .csvs were extracted using HOBOware Pro.
